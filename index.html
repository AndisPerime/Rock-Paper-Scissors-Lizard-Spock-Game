Bang Out!

Bang Out is an extended version of the classic Rock, Paper, Scissors game, created by Sam Kass and Karen Bryla to reduce ties. This our team version about this game. At this game you will meet Bang, which will try beat you in the game. The game is simple, you just need to choose one of the five options and see if you win, lose or tie. The game will keep track of the score and you can reset it at any time.

Instruction

  1. Click on the "Play Game" button to start the game.
  2. Click one of the five buttons to select your move.
  3. The result will be displayed, indicating who won or if it was a tie.
  4. The score will update accordingly.
  5. Click on the "Reset" button to reset the game.

Rules

  • Scissors cuts Paper
  • Paper covers Rock
  • Rock crushes Lizard
  • Lizard poisons Spock
  • Spock smashes Scissors
  • Scissors decapitates Lizard
  • Lizard eats Paper
  • Paper disproves Spock
  • Spock vaporizes Rock
  • Rock crushes Scissors
