About Us

Why we made the Bang Out Game

Inspired by the classic rock, paper, scissors, we've added an exciting twist with lizard and spock. Our team has implemented cutting-edge JavaScript to bring you a seamless gaming experience. Test your skills with varying levels of difficulty and enjoy an engaging design that keeps you coming back for more. At the heart of our project is a spirit of collaboration, bringing together innovative minds to create something truly extraordinary.
